About the project

The project, "Access to Drinking Water," is a project that focuses on analyzing access to safe and affordable drinking water, with a specific emphasis on inequalities in service levels between different countries and regions. The project is aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 6, which aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all. The data for this project is sourced from the WHO/UNICEF Joint Monitoring Programme for water supply, sanitation, and hygiene (JMP), which estimates household access to drinking water services between 2000 and 2020 to assess progress toward achieving the sixth SDG.


Project goal

Analyzing the provided data using Google Sheets, gathering insights, and addressing questions related to access to safe and affordable drinking water. The project focuses on inequalities in service levels between different countries and regions, emphasizing the importance of the analysis in the context of the SDG 6 targets.
